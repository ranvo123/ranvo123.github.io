Realistic setup

How to actually launch this

Create your Etsy seller account

Go to etsy.com/sell, pick a shop name, set country/currency, and add a payout bank account. Only you can do this — it needs your identity and banking details.

Make listing mockups

Buyers want to see art in a room. Drop each print into a free framed-wall mockup (Canva has them) for your listing photos — this matters more than anything for sales.

Upload each set as a digital download

Each folder in /products is one listing. Zip the folder with HOW-TO-PRINT.md, set listing type to "Digital," and attach the zip.

Paste the listing copy

Open the matching file in /listings — title, all 13 tags, and the full description are written and SEO-ready.

Price with the calculator above

Digital art runs ~85%+ margin. Set prices so net profit clears your target, then point the "View on Etsy" buttons (search data-etsy) to your live listings.

An honest note. No one can guarantee a store is "profitable" — that depends on your listings, photos, traffic, and pricing, not on this site. What's here gives you the best realistic shot: high-margin digital products people actually buy, professional listings, and accurate fee math. Your listing mockup photos are the single biggest driver of sales on Etsy — invest time there. Always verify current Etsy fees in your own Shop Manager.
